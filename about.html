The Code

Family Explorer has been developed in C# using WPF and loosely following the MVVM model. If you look into the code, you will likely find a lot of strange, un-advisable and downright faulty code. As an inexperienced and self-taught developer, this is inevitable, so please be supportive and give me your ideas for corrections and improvements.

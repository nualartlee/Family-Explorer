Quick Start

Open Family Explorer

Once you have installed Family Explorer, look on the desktop or in your programs list for the icon and click it to start.

If you have already been using Family Explorer and have files saved, you can just double-click on any of the files to open it directly.

Add Members

When you open Family Explorer for the first time, you will have a default member to start the family off. This member will already be selected and you will see the details on the left window.

Add Additional Relationships Between Existing Members

You might want to record a particular type of relationship between two members that has not already been created. For example, a father and son with have a parent relationship by default, but you might want to record a very close relationship between them as a friendship. To make this kind of record, right-click on one of the individuals to open the context menu. Click on 'Select' and from the submenu select the type of relationship you would like to add. The cursor will indicate that you are adding this relationship and will ask you to click on the reciprocal.

Print & Save

Print the relationship tree whole, or zoom/pan to a section of interest. Save each tree in an individual file for easy archiving.
